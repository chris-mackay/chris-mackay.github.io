Elevate Bio

Post Building Fit-out Project - Waltham, MA

Mechanical design engineer of the offices, laboratory area, and warehouse for the project. The office is a 26,000-sf open office design with exposed utilities. Moderna Therapeutics

Clinical Manufacturing Expansion Project - Norwood, MA

Mechanical design engineer for the 200,000-sf manufacturing facility. Provided the design and coordination of the ductwork for the HVAC systems. Also responsible for the drafting and design document production of the HVAC systems for the project. This project consisted of 16 rooftop air handing units for the clinical areas on the first level and 7 rooftop air handling units for the pre-clinical areas on the second level. The office spaces and cafeteria were served with packaged DX rooftop units. The cooling system consisted of (2) 700 ton chillers and open-cell cooling towers. This project was designed and modeled in Revit 2015. Navisworks 2015 was utilized for coordination between all disciplines.

Boston College

2000 Commonwealth Avenue Student Housing - Brighton, MA

Mechanical engineer for the renovation of an existing 224,000 sf 17-story high-rise apartment complex into a student housing facility. This effort added new mechanical ventilation via four new energy recovery units located on the roof. All rooftop bathroom and kitchen exhaust fans were removed, and existing exhaust duct systems were connected to new energy recovery units. The renovation also required dedicated cooling for the two new student lounges on the 2nd and 17th floors, which was accomplished with ductless split systems. The building was not originally built to accommodate the new ventilation duct system so careful coordination between all disciplines was essential to ensure all goals were successfully achieved.

Rochester School District

Elementary School Additions and Renovations - East Rochester, NH

Mechanical design engineer for the renovation of an existing elementary school. Worked alongside the project engineer to design the ductwork and piping systems. This project included 35,000 sf of new addition consisting of classrooms, an art room, music room, computer lab, media center, and administration spaces. Renovation of the existing 6,700 sf kindergarten building was also included. The systems implemented within the classrooms were passive chilled beams for cooling and perimeter linear displacement diffusers for ventilation and heating. The computer lab, media center, and administration spaces were provided with active chilled beams for cooling and ventilation and perimeter fin-tube radiation for heating. Systems implemented within the renovated kindergarten building were DX fan coil units for cooling coupled together with energy recovery ventilators for ventilation.
